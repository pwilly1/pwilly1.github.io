Flying Squirrel Types

Flying Squirrel Type Characteristics Habitat
Southern Flying Squirrel Small, grey-brown fur, glides using a membrane called a patagium Deciduous and mixed forests in North America
Northern Flying Squirrel Brown fur, slightly larger than the southern species, nocturnal Boreal and mixed forests in North America
Siberian Flying Squirrel Light grey fur, glides efficiently, large dark eyes Boreal forests in Northern Europe and Asia
