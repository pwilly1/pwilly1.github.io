Cats

Cats usually sleep about three fourths of their life. They don't sleep for long periods of time like us though they sleep for a few hours and go back to sleep a few hours later throughout the day.

Cats have bee ndomesticated for thousands of years and they have remained a beloved pet in many familys. Although cats are smaller than other pets like dogs they make up for this with their interesting and fun nature by engaging in play involves activities that mimic stalking, pouncing, and chasing, behaviors. This can result in some interesting and fun play times as your see them dart from place to place in an instant!

--Cat Facts--

  • There are cats who have more than 18 toes.
  • Cats can jump up to six times their length
  • Despite popular belief, many cats are actually lactose intolerant
